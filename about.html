Our Story

Founded in 2005, Hotel Paradise has been providing exceptional hospitality to guests from around the world for nearly two decades. What began as a modest establishment with just 20 rooms has now evolved into a luxury hotel with 150 rooms and suites, each designed with the utmost attention to detail and comfort.

Our vision was simple: create a space where guests can experience the perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and authentic hospitality. Today, that vision continues to drive everything we do, from the design of our rooms to the training of our staff.

Over the years, Hotel Paradise has received numerous accolades for its outstanding service, innovative design, and commitment to sustainability. But our greatest achievement is the loyalty of our guests, many of whom return year after year, becoming part of the Hotel Paradise family.
